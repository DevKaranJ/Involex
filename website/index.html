Beta Program FAQ

Everything you need to know about joining our beta program

Is the beta version free?

Yes! The beta version is completely free and includes all features. Beta users will get lifetime access at no cost.

How stable is the beta version?

Our beta is feature-complete with 113 passing backend tests. While we call it beta, it's production-ready and reliable.

What practice management systems are supported?

We currently support Cleo, Practice Panther, and MyCase with more integrations planned based on user feedback.

How do I provide feedback?

Join our GitHub discussions, report issues, or reach out directly. Your feedback helps shape the future of Involex.

Will my data be safe?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption and are fully GDPR compliant, even in beta.

When will the full version launch?

We're continuously improving based on beta feedback. There's no rush - beta users get all features forever free.
